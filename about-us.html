About us

Vision

It can be said that considering the history of a land as a flow, those hundred-year-old works are cultural sediments, moreover, cultural sediments are not easily obtained. It always accumulates a lot of elements of the past periods, playing a role in creating historical emotions, creating features and creating the vitality of a land. Except for construction in new lands, in general, all urban developments are integrated and add to a living body. Therefore, always respect and rely on what is available, stemming from historical factors, traditional culture, which creates the appearance and identity of the place. Conservation and development have conflicting points, but there is an organic relationship with each other and will support each other when there is a good solution. The case must be abolished is reluctant.

Mission

There are many such works, they themselves are not monuments. because they do not contain enough elements to become monuments independently and must be preserved in a holistic manner; But it is the factors that constitute the heritage of a land, a city. It serves as an important component of the spatial structure and the historical landscape has stabilized over time, creating a distinctive appearance and character of the region, urban, and region. Of course, not all buildings are old, but it is not difficult to recognize those valuable works. Once you recognize those works, you need to have a more general and global view. Need to step back a bit before moving towards a goal of development, to listen, to look deeply at a process of formation, existence, overall of the whole land. Since then will recognize the values ​​that are not labeled but very important to land.

Value

This is a non-physical element but very important. Therefore, when interfering with those works, it is necessary to look more broadly, listen to the opinions of the community. When the community speaks, it starts to touch the link and their interaction with the place. Therefore, if it is only for some development purpose, but destroying those works, it not only erases the memories of the land but also cuts the roots, breaks down the fulcrum of residents and attracts tourists. cross. Those things, how much development is not compensated. It is impossible to take a reason that is not a relic to destroy historical works. Those works are living bodies, with transformation over time, will stick with people, land so it needs to exist and develop. Therefore, it should be nurtured, not necessarily classified as a relic to fix it as a traces of a time.
